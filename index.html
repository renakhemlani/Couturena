‘Couturena‘ founded in  2021 by Rena Khemlani

This page is a place for me to post articles and information about sustainability and the fashion industry’s impact on the environment. As a person that loves fashion and enjoys forecasting trends and the constant excitement that comes with trends is having a drastic impact on our environment and health. I also want to use this platform to let those explore a bit about my life and journey.

The idea came to me when I started working for an NGO known as ‘Redress’. They have this wonderful platform that gives designers the opportunity to become sustainable designers. But a problem in my eyes is that future generations donâ€™t know enough about the impact of the fashion industry on our daily lives. If more content is publicized about sustainable fashion and also what is currently being developed it could make it easier for those to understand how to fix it.

I hope that through reading these posts you can learn more about textile waste but more importantly change for the greater good of the environment. My main reasoning for the name couturena is that I want runways and fashion brands to think of sustainability as the new couture. I want people to see through my eyes the changes we could make to this industry.
